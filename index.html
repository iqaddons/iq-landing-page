Features

Everything you need for Kuudra runs: supplies, build, stun, boss fight, and essential general utilities.

Pearl Waypoints

Precise waypoints for pearl throws, with timing/labels, stand block indicators and JSON config.

Supply Waypoints

Real-time beacons on supplies carried by giants, with customizable colors and automatic updates.

Pile Waypoints

Beacons on all crate piles, highlights "no pre" and automatically hides already completed piles.

Supply Timers

Shows pickup times for all players, with speed-based colors and a clean HUD overlay.

Supply Alerts

No Pre Alert, Already Picking title, Second Supply announce, custom Recover message and Giant hitbox alert.

Supply Progress Widget

Replaces the default title with a movable widget showing real-time 0/6 progress.

Build Progress Overlay

HUD with current %, fresh count, ETA and a color-coded progress bar.

Fresh Alerts & Timers

Fresh message in party chat (with %), timers above the fresher, personal countdown and highlight.

Build Helper Waypoints

Beacons on build piles with progress-based colors (red→green) and pile %.

Elle Highlight

Visible hitbox/outline on Elle during build phase for easier tracking.

Ballista Build Sound Replace

Replaces the default Ballista build sound with custom IQ audio.

Kuudra HP Bossbar

Custom bossbar with HP/%, colors and a damage calculator during the fight.

Stun Waypoints

Waypoints at ideal stun positions for consistent execution.

Kuudra Hitbox

Renders the hitbox with customizable outline and color, working through walls.

Block Useless Perks

Prevents accidentally buying useless perks (Steady Hands, Bomberman, Auto Revive and others).

Kuudra Direction Alert

Indicates which direction Kuudra will spawn from with a large title and colored indicator.

Rend Damage Tracker

Tracks when teammates land Rend, with damage value, timing and color-coded tiers.

Danger Zone Alert

Alerts when you are in danger zones (terracotta) with visual warning and sound.

Backbone Alert

Backbone timing with on-screen progress and Rend sync alert.

Hide Kuudra Damage Title

Hides the vanilla Kuudra damage title (e.g. 240M/240M) for a clean boss screen.

Custom Splits

Per-phase timing, total time, pace estimation and configurable thresholds with color grading.

Team Highlight

Highlights teammates with custom colors, distinguishing real players from NPCs.

Mana Drain Notify

Announces mana usage (Extreme Focus) in party chat with affected player count.

Party Join Sound

Plays a notification sound when someone joins your party.

Hide Mob Nametags

Prevents Kuudra mob nametags from rendering, reducing visual clutter.

Personal Best Tracker

Records your best Kuudra time and notifies you when you beat your PB.

Kuudra Profit Tracker

Calculates profit/loss per run with Bazaar/Auction pricing, keys/essence/books/armor and session totals.

Chest Utilities

Chest value display, 60-cap tracker, party reminders and Croesus helper (menus).

Auto Requeue

Automatically queues the next run after the boss, with configurable delay.

Kuudra Notifications

Notifications for key events: build/supplies/ichor/cannonball/reminders/phase change + sound.

Ability Announce

Announces selected casts: Spirit Spark, Hollowed Rush, Raging Wind, Ichor Pool, Mana Drain.

Party Commands

Commands via "!": warp/transfer/promote/kick, joininstance (t1–t5), ping/TPS and stats.

Wardrobe Keybinds

Instantly swap Wardrobe sets via keybinds with optional sound feedback.

Limbo Alert

Detects limbo kicks and automatically alerts the party.

Extra Visual Cleanup

Additional options to reduce clutter: hide vanilla bossbar and hide useless armor stands.

Showcase

A closer look at the UI, overlays, and gameplay enhancements that make every Kuudra run cleaner and faster.
